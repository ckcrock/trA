## üéØ TRADING STRATEGIES - QUICK GUIDE

### INTRADAY STRATEGIES (Hold: Minutes to Hours)

**1. VWAP Scalping** ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê
- Entry: Price touches VWAP + RSI confirm
- Target: 0.3-0.5%
- Stop: 0.2%
- Timeframe: 1-min, 3-min
- Best for: Liquid stocks, Nifty/BankNifty

**2. Opening Range Breakout** ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê
- Entry: Break of first 15-min high/low
- Target: 2x-3x OR range
- Stop: Opposite end of range
- Timeframe: 5-min
- Best for: Gap openings, volatile stocks

**3. EMA Crossover (9/21)** ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê
- Entry: 9 EMA crosses 21 EMA
- Target: 1-2%
- Stop: Below recent swing
- Timeframe: 5-min
- Best for: Trending days

**4. Supertrend + RSI** ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê
- Entry: Supertrend green + RSI > 40
- Target: Till Supertrend flips
- Stop: Below Supertrend line
- Timeframe: 3-min, 5-min
- Best for: Bank Nifty

**5. Bollinger Band Bounce** ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê
- Entry: Touch lower BB + RSI < 30
- Target: Middle BB
- Stop: Just below entry
- Timeframe: 15-min
- Best for: Range-bound markets

### SWING STRATEGIES (Hold: 2-10 Days)

**6. EMA Crossover (20/50)** ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê
- Entry: 20 EMA crosses 50 EMA + ADX > 25
- Target: 10-15%
- Stop: 2x ATR
- Timeframe: Daily
- Best for: Trending stocks

**7. RSI Divergence** ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê
- Entry: Price makes lower low, RSI makes higher low
- Target: Previous swing high
- Stop: Below divergence low
- Timeframe: Daily
- Best for: Reversals

**8. Fibonacci Retracement** ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê
- Entry: Pullback to 50% or 61.8% Fib
- Target: Previous high (or Fib extension)
- Stop: Below 78.6% Fib
- Timeframe: Daily
- Best for: Strong trends

**9. Supertrend + ADX** ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê
- Entry: Supertrend green + ADX > 25
- Target: Till Supertrend flips
- Stop: Supertrend line
- Timeframe: Daily
- Best for: F&O stocks

### LONG-TERM STRATEGIES (Hold: Months+)

**10. Golden Cross (50/200 SMA)** ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê
- Entry: 50 SMA crosses above 200 SMA
- Target: Long-term (months)
- Stop: 15-20% trailing
- Timeframe: Weekly
- Best for: Blue-chips, index funds

**11. Quality Growth Momentum** ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê
- Entry: ROE > 15% + Price > 200 SMA + Breakout
- Target: 1-3 years
- Stop: Below 200 SMA or fundamental deterioration
- Timeframe: Weekly/Monthly
- Best for: Wealth creation

## ‚öôÔ∏è RISK MANAGEMENT RULES

### Position Sizing
```
Position Size = (Capital √ó Risk%) / (Entry - Stop Loss)

Example:
- Capital: ‚Çπ100,000
- Risk: 2% = ‚Çπ2,000
- Entry: ‚Çπ500
- Stop Loss: ‚Çπ490
- Position Size = 2000 / 10 = 200 shares
```

### Stop Loss Guidelines
- **Intraday:** 0.2-0.5% (tight)
- **Swing:** 2-3% or 2x ATR
- **Long-term:** 8-10% or below 200 SMA

### Target Guidelines
- **Intraday:** 0.3-1% (quick)
- **Swing:** 5-15% (2:1 or 3:1 R:R)
- **Long-term:** 20-100%+ (hold winners)

### Capital Allocation
- Max per trade: 5-10%
- Max open positions: 5-10
- Max sector exposure: 40%
- Keep 20% cash reserve
